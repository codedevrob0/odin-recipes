Mama's Famous Italian Lasagna

Description

An italian classic that my Italian Momma used to make. Delicous and hearty this lasagna will bring back memories of Italy once you take a bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 Can of Roma Tomatoes
  • 1 Box of Lasagna
  • Olive Oil
  • 1/2 lbs. Ground Beef
  • 1/2 lbs. Ground Pork
  • 1 lbs. Ricotta Cheese
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Par-Boil the lasagna for 15 minutes.
  2. Put the can of Roma Tomatoes in a food processor and blend until a few chunks remain.
  3. Brown the ground beef and pork together in a skillet with Olive Oil. Add Salt and Pepper to taste.
  4. Drain the lasagna and layer the bottom of a rectangular dish with some of the lasagna.
  5. Put a thin layer of ground beef and pork.
  6. Put a thin layer of Ricotta Cheese on top of the meat.
  7. Cover the meat and cheese layer with a layer of tomatoe sauce.
  8. Add a layer of lasagna on top of that.
  9. Repeat this layering until all the ingredients are used.
  10. Bake the lasagna in the oven at 425 degrees for 1 hour or until all ingredients are cooked.
